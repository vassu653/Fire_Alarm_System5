    <h2>Project Overview</h2>
    <p>The Fire Alarm System is a safety innovation designed to detect and alert individuals in case of fire, smoke, or gas leaks. It ensures timely evacuation and minimizes property loss through real-time detection and notification mechanisms.</p>

    <h2>Objectives</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Detect fire or smoke quickly and accurately.</li>
      <li>Alert people through sound and light signals.</li>
      <li>Ensure safety and reduce damage to life and property.</li>
      <li>Provide an affordable, efficient fire detection solution.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Tools & Technologies Used</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Microcontroller (Arduino / Raspberry Pi)</li>
      <li>Smoke Sensor (MQ2 or Flame Sensor)</li>
      <li>Buzzer and LED Indicators</li>
      <li>Relay Module and Power Supply Unit</li>
      <li>Arduino IDE / Python / Embedded C</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Methodology</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Analyze requirements and select appropriate sensors.</li>
      <li>Design and assemble the circuit using a microcontroller.</li>
      <li>Write and upload the code for detection and alarm activation.</li>
      <li>Test under various fire and smoke conditions.</li>
      <li>Deploy and validate the system in real environments.</li>
    </ol>

    <h2>Output</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>The system instantly detects fire or smoke.</li>
      <li>The buzzer and LED indicators alert occupants.</li>
      <li>Optional IoT feature sends mobile notifications in emergencies.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Future Applications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Integration with smart home and industrial automation systems.</li>
      <li>AI-based fire prediction using environmental data.</li>
      <li>Cloud-based connection to emergency services.</li>
